There are several methods in which you can access the LOR directly. Outlined on this page is how to access it from a course that you have authoring rights to. At the top of this page you will find a narrated video demonstrating these steps. Below you will find the steps written with accompanying screenshots.

Step 1

From your Dashboard, click on a course.

Step 2

From the Navbar choose Tools

Step 3

Click “LOR” from the Tools Menu

Step 4

You are now on the main page for the LOR
