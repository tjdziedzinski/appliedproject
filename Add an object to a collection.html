If you find learning objects (either your own or your colleagues) you can make it easier to locate or organize them by placing them into collections. A collection is a group of learning objects that you create and manage. Learning objects can only be in one collection at a time.. At the top of this page you will find a narrated video demonstrating these steps. Below you will find the steps written with accompanying screenshots.

Step 1

Perform a search (A) or browse for a learning object. Once located, click on the object to view it (B).

Step 2

Click on the “More Actions” button

Step 3

Click “Add to Collection”

Step 4

Choose or create a new collection from the drop down menu

Step 5

Click the “Add” button

View a Collection
Step 1

From the Tools menu in the Navbar, click LOR

Step 2

On the right side under the heading “My Collections”, click on desired collection
