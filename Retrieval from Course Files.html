Importing directly into your course files can be a simple way to store learning objects for later use. When you use this method you are not adding the object into the content of your course, but doing so can make it easier to include it in the future. View the video above for a demonstration of how to complete this task. Below you will find step-by-step instructions accompanied by screenshots.

Step 1

From your Dashboard, click on a course.

Step 2

From the Navbar click "Admin"

Step 3

Click on "Course Admin"

Step 4

Under “Site Resources” click “Manage Files”

Step 5

Click “Add Learning Object” on the right

Step 6

Search (A) or browse (B) for a learning object

Step 7

Select a learning object of your choice (A)

Click "Next" button (B)

Step 8

Change the destination folder if necessary (A)

Then, click the “Copy” button (B)

Note: This method does not allow you to create a dynamic or dynamic locked link to a learning object. Only a copy content retrieval method is used.
