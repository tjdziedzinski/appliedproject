View the video above for a demonstration of how to complete this task. Below you will find step-by-step instructions accompanied by screenshots.

Step 1

From your Dashboard, click on a course.

Step 2

From the Navbar choose Tools

Step 3

Click “LOR” from the Tools Menu

Step 4

Search or browse for a learning object and click on the title to open it.

Step 5

Click on the “Retrieve” button.

Step 6

Choose how you would like to include the object into your course

Step 7

Choose the module in your course where this learning object will be apart of (A), or create a new module (B).

When ready, click "Create Topic" (C)

Step 8

Wait for the retrieval process to complete.

Step 9

Click on one of three choices to take you elsewhere in your course, or click “Done” to return back to the LOR.
