Publishing an object directly from your Course Files can be useful when either the object is not in your content, or if it is more easily accessible there. View the video above for a demonstration of how to complete this task. Below you will find step-by-step instructions accompanied by screenshots.

Step 1

From your Dashboard, click on a course.

Step 2

From the Navbar click "Admin"

Step 3

Click on "Course Admin"

Step 4

Under “Site Resources” click “Manage Files”

Step 5

Browse for the object you wish to publish.

To the right of the object’s title, click the “Action” button.

Step 6

Click “Publish to LOR”

Step 7

Choose what repository you will be publishing to or if you will be overwriting another object. (A)

 

Click "Next" button (B)

Note:

Overwriting an object is reviewed later in this module. Click here to access it. We will continue these steps by publishing the object as new.

Step 8

Fill out the required metadata fields including: title, description, and keywords that describe and categorize your learning object.

Click here to access the module on metadata.

Step 9

Click “Publish to LOR”

Step 11

After the process completes, click “Done” to return to your course files page.
