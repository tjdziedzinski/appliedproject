You may also find it useful to be able to retrieve learning objects while editing directly in the content of your course. View the video above for a demonstration of how to complete this task. Below you will find step-by-step instructions accompanied by screenshots

Step 1

From your Dashboard, click on a course.

Step 2

Click on Content in the Navbar

Step 3

Click on an existing module or create a new module

Step 4

Click the “Upload/Create” button

Step 5

Choose “Add Object from LOR”

Step 6

Search or browse for a learning object

Step 7

Select a learning object, then click "Next"

Step 8

Choose how you will retrieve the object

Step 9

Click “Create Topic” to initiate the retrieval
