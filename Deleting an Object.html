It may be appropriate to remove a learning object from the LOR, but careful consideration must be made to the effects it may have. When you delete an object, the entire object and all of it’s previous versions (if any) will be deleted. If the object is linked to other course (especially those that are not yours), access to the object will be removed. Deleting an object is also and irreversible action. At the top of this page you will find a narrated video demonstrating these steps. Below you will find the steps written with accompanying screenshots.

Step 1

From your Dashboard, click on a course.

Step 2

From the Navbar choose Tools

Step 3

Click “LOR” from the Tools Menu

Step 4

Search for an object (A) or select an object from “My Objects” on the right (B)

Click on the object once you locate it

Step 5

Click on the “More Actions” button

Step 6

Click “Delete”

Step 7

A confirmation dialog window appears, click "Yes" to continue

Immediately after the object is deleted, and you are returned to the main page of the LOR.

Step 8

Multiple objects can also be deleted at once from the results list.

Place a check mark next to each object (A) and clicking the delete button (B).
