Alternatively you can import learning objects from within the Course Admin page. This may be useful when you are importing multiple or large objects like modules into your course. View the video above for a demonstration of how to complete this task. Below you will find step-by-step instructions accompanied by screenshots.

Step 1

From your Dashboard, click on a course.

Step 2

From the Navbar click "Admin"

Step 3

Click on "Course Admin"

Step 4

Under the heading “Site Resources” click the “Import / Export / Copy Components” button

Step 5

Select “Import Components”

Step 6

Select “From Learning Object Repository” (A)

Then, click "Start" button (B)

Step 7

Search (A) or browse (B) for a learning object

Step 8

Select a learning object from the results list (A)

Click "Next" button (B)

Step 9

Choose the method in which you wish to retrieve the object, the click the "Next" button

Step 10

Choose the Parent Module (A) or create a new module (B) for the learning object to be put into.

Click "Create Topic" to finish (C)
